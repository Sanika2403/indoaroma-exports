Our Export Process 🌍

1. Sourcing & Harvesting

Spices are sourced directly from certified Indian farmers across Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu.

2. Cleaning & Grading

Raw spices undergo cleaning, drying, and grading to meet international export standards.

3. Quality Certification

Each batch is tested and certified by FSSAI and the Spice Board of India for purity and safety.

4. Packaging & Labeling

Spices are packed in hygienic, export-grade packaging to preserve freshness and aroma.

5. Customs & Shipping

Shipments are processed through customs and delivered globally via sea or air cargo.
